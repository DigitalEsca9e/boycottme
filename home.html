About Us


Our team has decided to create a browser extensions called “Boycott.me”. We got the idea for the app from the discussion we had in class. Boycotts are one of the most powerful methods of controlling large companies, that we as consumers have. Nothing hurts a company more than taking a hit to its bottom line. So we decided to create an app that would help in that process. People usually participate in a boycott when the news is recent and fresh on their minds. But in a fairly short amount of time people will forget about the boycott for the most part. Our app plans to remedy that. Most large companies that do get boycotted can usually afford to take the short term hit while they wait for people to forget. Our Extension helps users remember and track active boycotts.

The app works as an extension to inform you about the website you are visiting if you have boycotted the website. It also tracks news and displays it for your personal boycotting needs. The extension is also availiable on IOS and Android with the same functionalities
